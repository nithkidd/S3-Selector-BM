ABOUT US


FORD Division also known as Ford Cambodia is one of the biggest division operated by RMA Cambodia, who is member of RMA Group, a Leader in Global Business Solutions and a Diversified Company Providing Essential Products and Services to Economies in Transition. RMA Cambodia has earned a powerful reputation as a provider of retails and fleet solutions and for service in Cambodia as the Authorized Exclusive Distributor of World Class Automotive Brand of Ford Motor Company since 1997 and employing more than 200 people (full time employees). Ford Cambodia have 3 Main World-Class Facilities: 3 Dealer Showrooms, Spare Parts, and Service Centers in Phnom Penh & Siem Reap and One Quick Lane Service Center (Service All Makes & Models) and One Trade-In Showroom (Along the ways to Phnom Penh Airport. Local presence and knowledge of the market make RMA Cambodia reliable partners and trusted custodians of the best automotive products anywhere. Ford is the car brand most preferred by Cambodian and expat customers for business and family use. With our line of vehicles from small sedans and sport utility vehicles to pick-up trucks. Ford is also the top choice amongst government institutions and non-governmental organizations. In fact, RMA Cambodia provides all types of purpose-built vehicles at the highest brand standard levels as a “Qualified Vehicle Modifier” (QVM) for Ford Motor Company. Our conversion vehicles are for the mining and extraction sector, for firefighting units, troop carrier needs, ambulance fleets, and more. To round out our product and service portfolio for Ford and to be responsive to our customers’ needs, RMA Cambodia’s Ford service centers in Phnom Penh and Siem Reap are equipped with state-of-the-art testing technology and trained technicians to guarantee the best after-sales service in Cambodia.
